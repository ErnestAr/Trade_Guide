Stocks

A stock represents a share in ownership or equity of a corporation. Stock owners are entitled to a portion of the corporation’s assets and earnings. The portion depends on how much shares of that stock are owned.

The stock market is regulated by federal agencies such as, the US Securities and Exchanges Commission (SEC).

The stock market is operational from Monday to Friday, 9:30AM EST to 4:00PM EST.

Crypto

Cryptocurrency (or “crypto”) is a digital currency that is bought using real currency, and can be used to purchase goods and services.

There are no governing bodies or central authority regulating the cryptocurrency market.

The cryptocurrency market is operational 24/7, 365 days a year.
